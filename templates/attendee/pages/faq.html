Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about EventHub

How do I book tickets for an event?

Booking tickets is easy! Simply follow these steps:

  • Browse or search for events on our homepage
  • Click on the event you're interested in
  • Select the number of tickets you want
  • Choose your preferred ticket type (if available)
  • Proceed to checkout and complete payment via M-Pesa
  • You'll receive a confirmation email with your QR code tickets
How do I get my tickets after booking?

After successful payment, you'll receive your tickets in two ways:

  • Email: A confirmation email with QR code attachments
  • Dashboard: Your tickets are saved in "My Tickets" section
  • Mobile app: Access tickets on the go via our mobile app

Simply show the QR code at the venue for entry - no printing required!

Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?

Yes, ticket transfers are available for most events. You can transfer tickets by:

  • Going to "My Tickets" in your dashboard
  • Selecting the ticket you want to transfer
  • Clicking "Transfer Ticket" and entering the recipient's email
  • The recipient will receive an email to accept the transfer

Note: Some events may have restrictions on ticket transfers. Check the event details page for specific policies.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept M-Pesa as our primary and only payment method to make booking convenient and accessible for everyone in Kenya.

How to pay with M-Pesa:
  • Select M-Pesa as your payment method at checkout
  • Enter your M-Pesa registered phone number
  • You'll receive a STK Push prompt on your phone
  • Enter your M-Pesa PIN to complete the transaction
  • Payment confirmation is instant

All payments are processed securely through the official M-Pesa API. We never store your M-Pesa details.

How do I pay using M-Pesa?

Paying with M-Pesa is simple and secure:

  • At checkout, select "M-Pesa" as your payment method
  • Enter your M-Pesa registered phone number (e.g., 0712345678)
  • Click "Pay Now" - you'll receive a STK Push on your phone
  • Open your phone and enter your M-Pesa PIN
  • Wait for the confirmation message on both your phone and the website
  • Your tickets will be available immediately after successful payment
Note: Make sure your phone has sufficient balance or an active M-Pesa account.
What if my M-Pesa payment fails?

If your M-Pesa payment fails, here's what to do:

  • Check your phone for any STK Push message
  • Ensure you have sufficient balance in your M-Pesa account
  • Make sure your phone number is correctly entered
  • Wait 2-3 minutes - sometimes payments take a moment to process
  • If still unsuccessful, try again with a different phone number or check back later

If money is deducted but tickets are not received, contact our support team immediately with the M-Pesa transaction ID.

Can I get a refund for my tickets?

Refund policies vary by event and organizer. Generally:

  • Free events: No refund needed
  • Paid events: Check the event's refund policy on its page
  • Canceled events: Full automatic refund to your M-Pesa account within 5-7 business days
  • Rescheduled events: Tickets remain valid for new date or refund available

For refund requests, please contact the event organizer directly or reach out to our support team with your booking reference.

Is it safe to pay with M-Pesa on EventHub?

Absolutely! Your security is our top priority:

  • Official M-Pesa API: We integrate directly with Safaricom's official payment gateway
  • SSL Encryption: All data is encrypted using 256-bit SSL
  • No Storage: We never store your M-Pesa phone number or PIN
  • Secure STK Push: You approve payments directly from your phone
  • Transaction Records: You receive SMS confirmation from M-Pesa instantly

Your M-Pesa PIN is never shared with us - it's always entered on your own phone.

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is quick and free! Follow these steps:

  • Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner
  • Fill in your name, email address, and password
  • Agree to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
  • Click "Create Account"
  • Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox

Once verified, you can start booking events immediately!

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Resetting your password is easy:

  • Click "Login" in the top right corner
  • Click "Forgot Password?" below the login form
  • Enter your registered email address
  • Check your email for a password reset link
  • Click the link and create a new password

Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the email within a few minutes.

How do I update my profile information?

You can update your profile anytime:

  • Log into your account
  • Click on your profile icon in the top right
  • Select "Profile Settings" from the dropdown
  • Update your name, phone number, or other details
  • Click "Save Changes"

Some information like your email address may require verification before updating.

How do I find events near me?

Finding local events is simple:

  • Use the search bar at the top of the page
  • Enter your city or location in the search field
  • Filter by date, category, or price range
  • Browse through the curated list of nearby events
  • You can also save your location for personalized recommendations

Enable location services for automatic local event discovery!

How do I contact the event organizer?

Each event page includes organizer contact information:

  • Scroll to the "Organizer Information" section on the event page
  • You'll find the organizer's name, email, and sometimes phone number
  • Click "Contact Organizer" to send a direct message through our platform
  • You can also reach out to our support team, and we'll help connect you

For urgent issues during an event, check the event page for on-site contact details.

What happens if an event is canceled or rescheduled?

We'll notify you immediately if any changes occur:

  • Canceled Events: Full refund automatically processed to your M-Pesa account within 5-7 business days
  • Rescheduled Events: Your tickets remain valid for the new date, or you can request a refund
  • Venue Changes: Updated venue information will be sent via email
  • You'll receive email and in-app notifications about any changes
  • Check your dashboard's "My Bookings" for the latest status

Our support team is available to help with any event changes.
